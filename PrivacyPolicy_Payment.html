PERSONAL INFORMATION

You become a User when you access the Site, pursuant to Levanto’s Terms of Use. Except as required by law or requested by regulatory authorities, Levanto agrees to maintain your non-public information in the strictest confidence. Levanto restricts access to non-public personal information about Users to certain qualified Levanto employees who require that information in order to maintain and operate the Site. Levanto may ask you for additional personal information, such as your name or picture, in order to provide personalized services to you. Levanto stores, processes, and maintains data related to you in order to provide its products and services through the Site. Levanto’s uses for this data are described below. When you use the Site, Levanto’s servers automatically record certain information about your use of the Site. Similar to other web services, Levanto records information such as browsing activity, data displayed or clicked on (such as UI elements, ads, and links), and other information (such as browser type, IP address, date and time of access, cookie ID, and referrer URL). Along with cookies, Levanto may also use third-party tracking technology, such as Google Analytics, to record similar information regarding you and your activity on the Site. Levanto is not directed to anyone under the age of 18. If a parent or guardian becomes aware that his or her child under the age of 18 has provided us with personally identifiable information without his or her consent, he or she should contact us at info@levantofinancial.com. If we become aware that a child under the age of 18 has provided us with personally identifiable information, we will delete such information from our files.

USE OF INFORMATION

Levanto maintains and processes your Levanto account and its contents to provide Levanto’s products and services to you and to improve our services. Levanto’s servers process the information you provide to Levanto for various purposes, including but not limited to formatting and displaying your information, delivering related content, sharing community information, and other purposes relating to the Site. If you are a Client, Levanto may also process information for directing bill payments, transfers, or account activity, or for reporting your cash flow plan and performance. Levanto may send you information related to Levanto services, or your account if you are a Client. If you decide at any time that you no longer wish to receive marketing communications from us, please follow the “unsubscribe” instructions provided in the communications.

You understand and agree that any personal information that you provide through the Site and explicitly agree to share publicly might be made publicly available in Levanto’s discretion, and that other Users may use and reproduce such. We have no control over the use by other Users of the information that you voluntarily disclose to Levanto for this purpose or to the public in general. Levanto maintains physical, electronic and procedural safeguards that comply with federal standards to guard Users’ non-public personal information.

If you wish to become a Client, we will ask you for certain information, including, but not limited to, your full legal name, contact information, birth date, SSN, citizenship, income, expenses, approximate net worth, and other disclosures that may be necessary.
